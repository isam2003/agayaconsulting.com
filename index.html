What I Offer

Greetings! I bring over 15 years of finance expertise from my career with global institutions*, where I honed my skills in data analysis and translating vast sets of qualitative and quantitative data into sound financial forecasts and investment decisions. My journey in analyzing and reporting data has always been intertwined with decision-making. This experience seamlessly blends into my current passion: crafting tailored Business Intelligence (BI) solutions.

I specialize in creating customized reporting frameworks using low-cost or no-cost BI tools. By partnering closely with clients to understand their unique needs and budget constraints, I work my magic to develop BI frameworks that fit like a glove. However, I don't stop at creation; I'm dedicated to empowering my clients with the skills and knowledge to manage it themselves because their success is my measure of success.

Beyond reporting, I delve into KPIs, process automation, effective communication strategies, and data visualizations that inform, educate, and guide stakeholder buy-ins.

If you're seeking a budget-friendly, tailored BI framework coupled with a side of empowerment and data awesomeness, then I'm your go-to person!
