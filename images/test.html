About
Hi, I’m Indu. You’re driving change—but decisions are harder when data is scattered, unclear, or outdated. We know nonprofit teams are stretched thin. I’ve worked with incredible organizations that change lives—but too often, they’re stuck guessing what’s working. That’s where we come in.

We help nonprofits and mission-driven teams build dashboards, reports, and systems that make insight easy and action faster. Whether you're just getting started or scaling your analytics, we’ll meet you where you are—and build what you need to go further.

What we help you do
We build data solutions with you—not just for you. Every tool, dashboard, or system we design is shaped in partnership with your team, using plain language and built-in guardrails to make sure you can run with it confidently. Your independence is our definition of success.

  • Create a clear analytics roadmap so you know what to prioritize
  • Set up data systems that work reliably across teams and tools
  • Build interactive dashboards and reports that are easy to use
  • Train your staff so they can explore and answer their own data questions

Because the real value of data isn’t in collecting it—it’s in using it.
